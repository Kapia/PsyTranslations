Language solutions: More than a medical translation

I deliver more than just a translation. I help you convey your message loud and clear to a global market of over 489 million Spanish speakers.

I guarantee a bespoke service that will save you time and money, freeing you up to focus on other strategic objectives. As a psychologist and English to Spanish medical translator, I have extensive experience and specialist training in medical translation, psychology, education, and other areas of health sciences.

Highly specialized disciplines call for highly specialized translations.

Those working in health sciences and psychometric assessment will be keenly aware of the requirement to comply with strict professional guidelines. They also know that any translated content must be of the highest quality and must maintain the principles of validity, reliability, and fairness with respect to the original.

These key principles underpin all translated documents, be they:

  • psychometric instruments for clinical use or for human resource management
  • questionnaires on patient-reported outcomes in clinical trials
  • leaflets and documentation for patients
  • instructions for use for medical devices
  • reports on curriculum accommodations
  • scientific articles
